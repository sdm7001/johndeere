# WAM 110.16 - Clean Up Labor

Clean up labor is only allowed when there is loss of fluid and washing the area is necessary to complete the repair.

## Allowed

- Local cleaning tied to a fluid leak or fluid loss.
- Cleaning needed to complete or verify the repair area.

## Not allowed

- Washing the entire machine as preparation for warranty repair.
- Washing the entire machine after warranty repair.
- General cleanup not tied to fluid loss.

## Notes

- OEM-only repairs have a one hour maximum cleanup labor limit per repair.
- Dealer operating rule for this MVP flags normal cleanup above 0.5 hr for review unless a source-specific rule applies.
