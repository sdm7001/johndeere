# MST Flat-Rate Source Register

Official Deere MST or flat-rate guides are required before the app can confirm allowed job times.

## Current status

- Status: pending official source registration.
- The app must not invent MST times.
- If MST time is requested but the official guide is unavailable, the app should flag the claim for manual review.

## Data needed

- Source name.
- Effective date or retrieval date.
- Applicable model or component scope.
- Operation or task description.
- Allowed time.
- Source attachment or official reference.
